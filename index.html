Welcome to ADHD Tools Hub

This website provides a collection of interactive tools designed specifically to help individuals with ADHD manage their symptoms, improve productivity, and enhance focus.

Pomodoro Timer

Work in focused sprints with timed breaks to maintain productivity.

Eisenhower Matrix

Prioritize tasks based on importance and urgency.

Day Planner

Visualize your day with time blocks for better time management.

Task Manager

Keep track of your to-do list with priorities and categories.

Task Breakdown

Break complex tasks into smaller, manageable steps.

Habit Tracker

Build consistency with daily habit tracking and streaks.

Routine Tool

Plan and execute sequences of tasks with timers.

Focus Mode

Minimize distractions with a clean, focused interface.

Rewards

Celebrate your accomplishments with visual rewards.
